Here's The Why

I opened my daycare because I could not find childcare for my son. He was passed around between family members and co-workers. One night, after work, I jumped into my vehicle and I could not remember where I left him. I had to call everyone I knew to see where he was. After I located him, I knew my current situation was not working. That is when I decided I needed to open my childcare so families would not have to go through what I experienced that night.
